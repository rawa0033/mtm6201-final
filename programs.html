Programs

Seniors

Active Life Center offers senior-friendly programs designed to keep you moving, connected, and inspired. From gentle yoga and aquafit to social events and accessible facilities, we make it easy to stay active at your own pace.

Family

At Active Life Center, we’re here for every member of your family. From kids’ swim lessons and summer camps to fitness classes parents can enjoy, we’ve created spaces and programs that bring families together.

Summer Camp

Fun-filled weekly camps for kids aged 6–12 with sports, swimming, crafts, and outdoor adventures. Runs July–August.

Gentle Yoga Classes

Gentle yoga offers a slow-paced, restorative practice with basic poses, breathing, and mindfulness to enhance relaxation and flexibility. It's ideal for seniors, beginners, or anyone seeking stress relief.

Jamboree Classes

Jamboree classes help moms bond with their toddlers through music, play, and fun activities that support social and motor skill development.

Pilates Classes

Pilates classes build core strength, flexibility, and body awareness through controlled movements using mats or equipment. They suit all fitness levels and focus on posture, balance, and precision.

Zumba Classes

Zumba classes are high-energy dance workouts that boost heart health through fun routines like salsa and hip-hop in a lively, social setting.

Popular Programs

Aquacise

Low-impact water fitness classes that build strength and flexibility, perfect for all levels. Stay active while enjoying a fun, social atmosphere in the pool.

Senior fit

Gentle fitness classes designed to improve strength, balance, and mobility for older adults. Stay healthy and active in a supportive, social environment.

Free Lunch Skate

Enjoy a free public skating session and a light lunch with family and friends. A perfect way to stay active and connect with your community every Friday.
